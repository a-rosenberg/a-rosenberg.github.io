red cabbage sesame slaw

gets better with time

ingredients

  • 1 large carrot; shredded
  • 2.5 cup red cabbage; thinly sliced
  • 3 green onion; thinly sliced, discarding the white parts
  • 2 Tbsp olve oil
  • 2 Tbsp sesame oil
  • 1" ginger root; finely minced
  • 2 garlic cloves; minced
  • 2 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 3 Tbsp tamari or soy sauce
  • some sesame seeds for topping

instructions

Mix it all together and let it sit for at least a half an hour. The longer the better. I used it on top of a forbidden rice bowl with roasted sweet potato and asparagus, but it would go well with fish or meat marinated in a any sesame/soy/miso/rice vinegar mixture.
