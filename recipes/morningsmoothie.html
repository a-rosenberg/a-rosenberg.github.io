morning smoothie

standard aaron breakfast

ingredients

  • 1 peeled, previously frozen banana
  • 1 Cup unsweetened plain almond milk
  • 2 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 Tbsp rolled oats
  • 1 tsp flax seed
  • 1/2 cup frozen spinach
  • 1 tsp spirulina powder (optional)

instructions

Add ingredients in order listed. Blend until smooth. Add additional almond milk or use a tamper if not flowing.

notes

Spirulina powder is ground up algae (actually a cyanobacteria), and while high in protein is expensive and to some an aquired taste. See if you can try a little bit of it from a pay by weight setion at your healthy food store before commiting to it.
